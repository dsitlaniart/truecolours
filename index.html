About Arpan Trust

Our partner — who your
bid supports

Arpan Trust is a certified non-profit organisation based in India, dedicated to empowering children and adults aged 6 to 35 with intellectual disabilities. For over three decades, Arpan has created a nurturing, inclusive space where individuals with multiple disabilities are supported through holistic education, therapeutic care, and skill development.

"We envision a world where every individual with special needs is not only accepted but celebrated — where their unique abilities shape inclusive communities."

Through special education, speech & language therapy, physiotherapy, sensory integration, art, music, sports, and vocational training, Arpan equips every beneficiary with tools to live an independent, fulfilling life.

How It Works

to lasting impact

Submission Requirements

What we expect from
every submission

—
Original Artwork Only
Must be entirely your own original work. No reproductions or AI-generated pieces. Any theme is welcome.
—
Medium Must Be Stated
Clearly specify the medium — oil, acrylic, watercolour, ink, mixed media, digital, sculpture, etc. Mandatory for all.
—
Dimensions & Starting Bid
Provide physical dimensions and suggest a starting bid in INR. Required for all submissions.
—
High-Quality Photography
The artwork and photograph must both be high quality. Even lighting, neutral background, minimum 2000px. Blurry or dark images will be rejected.
—
Verification Before Listing
All submissions are reviewed before going live. Our team may request additional photos or information.
—
Teachers: School & Location Required
If submitting as a teacher, your school name and city/country are mandatory fields.

Open to everyone. This auction is not limited to art students. Students of any subject, teachers, staff, and external artists from anywhere in the world are welcome. Encourage friends and colleagues to join.

Certificate of Contribution

Every artist whose work is verified and listed receives an official certificate of contribution from Arpan Trust. This is a meaningful addition to university applications, portfolios, and professional records — proof of real social impact.

Why participation matters

Contributing demonstrates social impact, community leadership, and global citizenship — qualities valued by universities worldwide. By participating, you directly fund education and therapy for children with intellectual disabilities.
